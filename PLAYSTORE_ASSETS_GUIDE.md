### 1. APP ICON (512√ó512 PNG)
**Specifications:**
- **Dimensions:** 512√ó512 pixels
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Requirements:**
  - No rounded corners (Google adds them automatically)
  - No text or app name
  - Should work on light and dark backgrounds
  - High contrast for visibility at small sizes
  - Must be distinctive and recognizable

**Design Tips:**
- Use church/faith imagery (cross, light, open book, hands in prayer)
- Colors: Deep teal (primary) with warm orange (secondary) accents - per church branding
- Avoid gradients if possible; solid colors work better at thumbnail size
- Test at 48√ó48 pixels to ensure readability

**File Location:** `assets/images/icons/app_icon.png`

### Free Design Tools
1. **Canva** (https://www.canva.com)
   - Easy screenshot/graphic templates
   - Built-in Play Store dimension templates
   - Free version available
   
2. **GIMP** (https://www.gimp.org)
   - Free, open-source image editor
   - Full control over designs
   
3. **Figma** (https://www.figma.com)
   - Professional design tool
   - Free tier available
   - Collaborate with designers

### Design Considerations
- **Brand Colors:**
  - Primary: Deep Teal (#1B5E75 or similar)
  - Secondary: Warm Orange (#E8934A or similar)
  
- **Fonts:** Sans-serif (Roboto, Open Sans) for readability
- **Icons:** Use material design icons or fontawesome
- **Imagery:** Church, faith, community, light, open books, prayer hands

### Alternative: Screenshots from Running App
You can also:
1. Run the Flutter app on Android emulator
2. Take screenshots directly from the app
3. Add text overlays in Canva or image editor
4. This ensures accuracy and authenticity
