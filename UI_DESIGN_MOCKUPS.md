## DESIGN SUMMARY

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Deep Teal (#1B7B7B) - Trust, faith, serenity
- **Secondary**: Warm Orange (#E8934A) - Warmth, community, welcome
- **Accent**: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) - Clean backgrounds
- **Text**: Dark Gray (#333333) - Readability

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, 18-20px
- **Body Text**: Regular, 14-16px
- **Buttons**: Semi-bold, 14px

### UI Elements
- **Cards**: Rounded corners (8px), subtle shadow
- **Buttons**: Rounded (6px), full-width or compact
- **Icons**: Material Design icons, 24px standard
- **Spacing**: 16px standard padding, 8px between elements

### Navigation
- **Bottom Navigation**: Always visible, 5 main sections
- **Hamburger Menu**: Additional options in "More" section
- **Back Button**: Available on detail pages

### Accessibility
- **Text Contrast**: WCAG AA compliant (4.5:1 minimum)
- **Font Sizes**: Readable at 16px minimum
- **Touch Targets**: 48x48px minimum (36x36px acceptable)
- **Color Independence**: Not relying solely on color for information
