# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 19, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 19, 2026

## 1. Introduction

New Life Community Church Tonyrefail ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the New Life Community Church Tonyrefail mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and safeguarding of information obtained from users ("you," "your," or "User") of the App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Prayer Requests & Contact Form**
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- Prayer request or message content
- Any other information you choose to include

These details are collected when you submit a prayer request or contact form through the App and are used solely for responding to your inquiry.

**User Reflections & Notes**
- Personal notes you add to devotions
- Favorite verses and Scripture selections
- Reflection content (stored locally on your device)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Unique device identifier (IDFA for iOS, Android Advertising ID)
- Device language and locale settings

**Usage Information**
- Features used within the App
- Content viewed (devotions, Bible verses, events)
- Frequency and duration of App usage
- Pages or features accessed
- Error messages and crash reports

**Location Information**
- The App does NOT collect precise GPS location data
- General location may be inferred from IP address for analytics purposes only

### 2.3 Information from Firebase & Push Notifications

**Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)**
- Device registration tokens for push notifications
- Notification delivery status and engagement metrics
- Timestamp of notification interactions

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send you:
- Daily devotion reminders
- Church announcements and updates
- Special event notifications
- Prayer alerts

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Providing App Services**
   - Delivering devotional content
   - Processing prayer requests and inquiries
   - Managing user preferences and settings
   - Storing your favorite verses and notes

2. **Communication**
   - Sending push notifications about devotions, events, and church updates
   - Responding to prayer requests and contact form submissions
   - Sending important service announcements

3. **Analytics & Improvement**
   - Understanding how users interact with the App
   - Identifying and fixing bugs or technical issues
   - Enhancing App features and user experience
   - Measuring engagement with content

4. **Safety & Security**
   - Detecting, investigating, and preventing fraud or security issues
   - Enforcing our Terms of Service and other agreements
   - Protecting the rights, property, and safety of New Life Church, Users, and the public

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- Your personal notes, favorite verses, and preferences are stored **locally on your device** only
- This data is NOT transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly share it
- Local data persists even if you uninstall the App (unless you clear app data)

### 4.2 Cloud Storage
- Prayer request information and contact form submissions are sent securely to our servers
- We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) to protect data in transit
- We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Prayer requests are retained for 12 months or until resolved
- Contact form submissions are retained for 6 months
- Push notification logs are retained for analytics purposes for up to 90 days
- You may request deletion of your personal data at any time (see Section 7)

---

## 5. Sharing Your Information

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### 5.1 Limited Sharing

We may share information in these limited circumstances:

1. **Service Providers**
   - Firebase (Google) for push notifications and analytics
   - Hosting providers for secure data storage
   - Email services for communication (if applicable)

2. **Church Leadership**
   - Prayer request details are shared with designated prayer team members only
   - Contact form submissions may be reviewed by church staff relevant to your inquiry

3. **Legal Requirements**
   - If required by law, court order, or government request
   - To protect the safety, rights, and property of New Life Church and Users

4. **Aggregated Data**
   - We may share anonymized, aggregated usage statistics with church leadership for ministry planning
   - This data cannot identify you personally

## 6. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

### 6.1 Notification Preferences
- You can manage push notification settings within the App
- You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time
- Disabling notifications will not affect other App functionality

### 6.2 Data Access & Deletion
- You can view your saved notes and favorite verses in the App
- You can delete individual notes, verses, or App data through your device settings
- You can request permanent deletion of your prayer requests and contact form submissions

### 6.3 Opting Out
- **Do Not Track Signals**: The App does not currently respond to Do Not Track browser signals
- **Analytics**: You may opt out of analytics by adjusting your device's tracking settings

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for general audiences and does NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account immediately.

For users ages 13-18, we recommend parental review of this Privacy Policy and parental supervision of App usage.
